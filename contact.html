Quick Answers

Common Contact Queries

What's the best way to get urgent help?

For urgent matters, especially concerning welfare or time-sensitive tax issues, please call us directly on 01254 405737 during office hours.

Is there a fee for an initial consultation?

We offer a free initial consultation to discuss your needs and how we can assist. This allows us to understand your situation and for you to learn about our services with no obligation.

What documents should I bring to my first appointment?

This depends on the service you require. When you book an appointment, we will advise you on any specific documents to bring. Generally, relevant financial statements, HMRC correspondence, or benefit letters are helpful.
